Our History

Happy Pet was born in 2010 from a simple dream: to create a place where animals are treated not just as pets, but as family members. What started as a small grooming service in a garage has now become a benchmark for animal care in the city.

Our team consists of animal lovers. Here, every "woof" and "meow" is heard with attention. We believe that animal well-being involves physical and mental health, and lots of affection.

Our Values

We work guided by three fundamental pillars:

  • Unconditional Love: We treat every animal with patience and respect.
  • Transparency: You can watch the entire grooming process through our glass windows.
  • Quality: We use only top-tier, hypoallergenic products.

Come visit us!

We love receiving visitors (especially the four-legged ones!). Come have a coffee with us and see our facilities.
